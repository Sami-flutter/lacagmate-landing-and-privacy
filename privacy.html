Privacy Policy

Last updated:

LacagMate collects basic information such as your name, email, password and profile picture when you create an account. Inside the app, your added income, expenses, and daily transactions are stored securely to generate balance reports.

Information We Collect

  • Your name and email
  • Your profile image
  • Your income and expense entries
  • Daily and monthly transaction data for reporting

How We Use Your Data

  • To create your account
  • To calculate your balance and daily reports
  • To display your transaction history inside the app

Data Security

All your information is stored safely. Passwords are encrypted and cannot be viewed by anyone. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.
